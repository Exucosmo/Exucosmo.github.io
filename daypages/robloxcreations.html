—— ROBLOX CREATIONS ——

I've been interested in making games for a good chunk of my life at this point. Quarantine enabled me to work on these little projects, but I couldn't seem to actually finish them. So here they are, basically a graveyard of unfinished games and unrealized ideas. It's honestly kinda sad to look at. But maybe one day I'll get to fully realize one of these.

There are definitely way more games than these, with sizeable place visits, but I'm frankly too embarassed to show them. They're silly. So the ones below are a list of what I'm confident enough to show off, basically.

ROBLOX-GAME.exe

Hamon

Apparently my biggest game on Roblox. How did this reach 4500+ visits???

"Hamon" is a power in JoJo's Bizarre Adventure. I was trying to make a fighting game of my own with this idea. Obviously it sucks, but you can fight using the keys: E (hold), R, T, F, and Z. Each attack has a cooldown. The first time you use an ability, it doesn't load the animation for some reason. So try it again to see the animation.

https://www.roblox.com/games/5840572730/Hamon

A small little library to relax in and maybe write something.
